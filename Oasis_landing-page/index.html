All about your friendly neighbourhood Spiderman

Movies

Spider-Man movies have brought the iconic superhero to life on the big screen in various cinematic universes, offering distinct portrayals of the character and his world. From Sam Raimi's original trilogy to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's take on Peter Parker, these films have captivated audiences with their different storytelling approaches and visual spectacles.

Animations

Spider-Man cartoons have spanned various animated series, offering diverse interpretations of the web-slinger's adventures, from classic Saturday morning cartoons to modern animated adaptations.
These shows explore different facets of Spider-Man's character, appealing to fans of all ages with their unique storytelling styles and artistic approaches.

Comics

Spider-Man exists in multiple parallel universes, each presenting a distinct version of the iconic character, from Peter Parker's mainstream adventures to alternate realities like the Ultimate Universe and the Spider-Verse.
These variations showcase the character's enduring appeal and versatility across different storytelling contexts.
